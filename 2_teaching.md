## NBER Heterogeneous-Agent Macro Workshop (2022)

Teaching assistant for the National Bureau of Economic Research's [**Heterogeneous Agent Macro Workshop**](https://www.nber.org/conferences/heterogeneous-agent-macro-workshop-spring-2022), co-taught with Adrien Auclert, Bence Bardóczy, Matthew Rognlie, and Ludwig Straub.
{: .teaching-meta}

Materials: [GitHub repository](https://github.com/shade-econ/nber-workshop-2022).
{: .teaching-resource}

Selected topics
{: .teaching-label}

- Solution methods for the standard incomplete markets (Bewley-Huggett-Aiyagari) model
- Canonical heterogeneous agent New Keynesian (HANK) model with nominal wage rigidities
- Topics in monetary and fiscal policy
- Likelihood-based estimation
