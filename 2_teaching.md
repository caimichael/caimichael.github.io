  <dt>Role</dt>

  <dt>Workshop</dt>
  <dd><a href="https://www.nber.org/conferences/heterogeneous-agent-macro-workshop-spring-2022"><strong>Heterogeneous Agent Macro Workshop</strong></a></dd>
  <dt>Co-taught with</dt>
  <dd>Adrien Auclert, Bence Bardóczy, Matthew Rognlie, and Ludwig Straub</dd>
</dl>

Materials: [GitHub repository](https://github.com/shade-econ/nber-workshop-2022).
{: .teaching-resource}

Selected topics
{: .teaching-label}

- Solution methods for the standard incomplete markets (Bewley-Huggett-Aiyagari) model
- Canonical heterogeneous agent New Keynesian (HANK) model with nominal wage rigidities
- Topics in monetary and fiscal policy
- Likelihood-based estimation
